Trace Bates Bio (Wiki)

Who is Trace Bates?

Trace Bates is a Reality star who has appeared on Up TV series Bringing Up Bates. He is the eighth son of Gil and Kelly Bates. Trace Bates is carrying on his family’s business and has also appeared on the TLC’s show, 19 Kids and Counting.

Trace Bates – Age, Childhood, Education

Trace was born on February 1, 1997, to Gil Bates and Kelly Jo Callaham in Tennessee. He is the eighth child out of 19. His ethnicity is English. His birth sign is Aquarius. His name was kept after the meaning “harvester of the white fields”.

He was homeschooled. He later graduated from high school in 2016.
