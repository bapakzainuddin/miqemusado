Tyler Perry's Partner Gelila Supports Girls Gotta Run Foundation in Her Philanthropic Work

Tyler Perry's long-time partner, Gelila Bekele, gave updates on her philanthropic work towards the Girls Gotta Run Foundation as she showed off a heartwarming snap on social media.

Tyler Perry's partner, Gelila Bekele keeps up her good works regarding philanthropy and she makes sure to let fans in on her moves.

Bekele looked stylish in a puffer jacket, a pair of form-fitting pants, and a baseball hat. While all the girls rallied around her, the philanthropist crutched with a big smile and a peace sign. According to its official page, Girls Gotta Run Foundation (GGRF) is the:

"... first organization for girls combining athletic abilities, a thirst for education, and how to have better communities in Ethiopia."

GGRF was established in 2006 as a non-governmental franchise to lend a helping hand to the education of the underprivileged girl child in Ethiopia. The organization centers on female runners who use their athletic abilities to help their communities.

Founded by Dr. Patricia Ortman, the non-profit has channeled funds into providing shoes, athletic gear, and other necessities for the runners. So far, there has been immense progress concerning the eradication of early marriage and improved education.
