Angel Brinks Married, Husband, Partner, Relationship &amp; Net Worth

The reality TV personality from Basketball Wives LA, who works as a fashion designer professionally, is Angel Brinks. Her eponymous line of fashion is focused on leggings, bodysuits, dresses, lingerie, rompers, denim, and jackets.

Angel Brinks was born on 19 October 1982 in Los Angeles, Califonia, USA, under the zodiac sign of Libra. From childhood, she became interested in the globe of fashion and glamour. In addition, at the age of three, she started taking ballet classes. 

The star holds American nationality and belongs to a mixed Armenian and Italian ethnic background. There is no comprehensive data on the academic background of Brinks. But she said she attended Night College in an interview with different sources and would work during the day.

Following her strong enthusiasm for fashion design, Angel went to performing arts but introduced her clothing line Angel Brink fashion in 2010 to bring her passion to reality.

Through her line of fashion, Angel can reach out to females who always want to look elegant, edgy, sexy, and yet confident. The most interesting fact is, she has featured the New York Fashion Week 2012 as well as the Fashion Show of Facet Studio 2013 and 2014. Her clothing line is still doing really good so far.
