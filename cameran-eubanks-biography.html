Cameran Eubanks Bio (Wiki)

Who is Cameran Eubanks?

Cameran Eubanks is a reality television star.

She is most recognized as a loveable cast member of the successful Bravo show, Southern Charm, but the show just isn’t her first rodeo in reality television.

She has starred in reality TV shows such as The Real World on MTV, The Challenge, and Watch What Happens: Live.

Cameran Eubanks: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Cameran Eubanks was born on November 21, 1983, in Anderson, South Carolina, USA.

At that time the Real World was filmed, she was described as a “Southern Belle” who loved hanging out with all the men and was always up to get a great prank.”

Her mother’s name is Bonnie Eubanks and her father’s name is Robert Eubanks. She has a sister, Cayce Eubanks and she does not have any brother.

Cameran grew up in a picture-perfect upper-class family but reach a low point when her parents divorced while she was in high school.

Cameran Eubanks also has a sister named Cayce Eubanks.

There are no details about her academics. Her ethnicity is Caucasian.

Cameran Eubanks: Career, Profession

After “The Real World: San Diego”, Cameran Eubanks started appearing in the show “Southern Charm” along with her co-stars Shep Rose, Thomas Ravenel, and others.

Well noted for the beauty and grace with which she carries herself in her shows, she has managed not only to gain fans and followers from her television appearances but has also earned wealth over the years.

Cameran Eubanks is also known for her love of pranks and hanging out with the boys. Apart from her first appearance on the show in 2004, she has also appeared in the two more reality shows of the MTV franchise such as Real World/Road Rules Challenges (“Battle Of The Sexes 2” and “The Gauntlet 2”).

She currently stars in the show “Southern Charm”.
