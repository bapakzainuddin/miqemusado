'Baywatch' Star Erika Eleniak Slams Paparazzi through Tears &amp; No Makeup in New Video

Erika Eleniak | Source: facebook.com/baywatch ; instagram.com/officialerikaeleniak

"Baywatch" star Erika Eleniak recently found herself in the paparazzi's crosshairs as her off-duty snapshots buzzed around the web. In response, the actress released an emotional video addressing both her fans and the paparazzi.

Sporting a casual T-shirt and hair tied back, Eleniak appears without makeup, her eyes glistening with tears. Her voice wavers as she clarifies that her tears stem not from sadness but overwhelming gratitude towards her supporters rallying around her.

The actress extended heartfelt thanks to her defenders after the internet was awash with articles about her rare outing, leading to an onslaught of critical comments about her appearance. This incident unfolded after a seemingly simple dog walk escalated with two suspicious vehicles tailing her.

Eleniak called out the media for their detrimental and intrusive articles, criticizing their exposure of her personal details. Fearful for her child's safety, she reminisced about her last birthday spent alongside law enforcement due to a stalker lurking near her home.

The star warned the paparazzi that she's prepared to return the favor next time, readying her camera to snap their photos and compare them with their decades-old images. To wrap up, Eleniak announced her upcoming podcast appearance where she will delve deeper into her experiences.

Eleniak felt compelled to share her video after snapshots of her dog-walk escapade circulated online. The 53-year-old actress, who once donned the iconic red swimsuit, now flaunts her skin canvas adorned with a myriad of tattoos.

For her casual dog-walk outing, the actress opted for a laid-back ensemble of a maroon tee, patterned pants, and flip-flops. The mother-of-one stepped out makeup-free, her youthful complexion and radiant natural beauty on full display.

Eleniak, who graced the "Baywatch" screen for its initial three seasons before the arrival of Pamela Anderson, is poised to feature in an upcoming documentary chronicling the iconic series. Confirmed for in-depth interviews alongside her are other prominent Baywatch figures including David Hasselhoff, Nicole Eggert, David Chokachi, Alexandra Paul, Gena Lee Nolin Hulse, Jason Simmons, and Jeremy Jackson.
