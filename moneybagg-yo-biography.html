Moneybagg Yo Bio (Wiki)

Who is Moneybagg Yo?

Moneybagg Yo is an American rapper and songwriter.

He works with Yo Gotti‘s record label, Collective Music Group (CMG), and with N-Less Entertainment.

Further, he owns the label Bread Gang Music Group also.

How many siblings does Moneybagg Yo have?

The rapper was born on September 22, 1991, in Memphis, Shelby County, Tennessee, United States. His birth name is Demario DeWayne White, Jr.

There is hardly any information available on the family background and his early life.

He belongs of American nationality and is of All-American ethnicity.

Demario completed his schooling at a local school in his hometown. He attends college but gets dropped out of high school in 12th grade.

Details regarding his education are unknown.

Moneybagg Yo: Career, Professional Life

On April 16, 2012, Moneybagg Yo released his first mixtape titled From Da Block 2 Da Booth via Bread Gang Entertainment.

The same year, he released another mixtape titled ‘October 20th’ with the label on October 6.

Moreover, for his next mixtape “La Familia“, the rapper collaborated with Slpy. It was released on February 3, 2014.

Demario then published a revision of the first installment of the Federal mixtape titled “Federal Reloaded“.

He also collaborated with Yo Gotti publishing the second installment of his Federal series “2 Federal”.

On June 7, 2016, he aired another mixtape titled “ELO” (Everybody Lives On) featuring Yo Gotti.

He has also worked along with OG Boo Dirty and Young Dolph. Meanwhile, he signed with ‘Collective Music Group,’ also referred to as ‘CMG.’ Moneybagg Yo’s next mixtape titled ‘Federal 3X’ was released on August 11, 2017.

Demario released his debut studio album, Reset, on November 2, 2018, via Collective Music Group, N-Less Entertainment, Bread Gang Entertainment, and Interscope Records.

The album is comprised of songs such as Chanel Junkie, Curry Jersey, Fall Down, In Her Voice, Tryna Do, Okay, Jungle, and They Madd.

The thirteenth mixtape and his 2nd studio album, 43va Heartless, was released on May 24, 2019, via Collective Music Group, Bread Gang Entertainment, N-Less Entertainment, and Interscope Records and it was placed at number #3 in the US Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums (Billboard) chart and at number #4 in the US Billboard 200 chart.

Reset was ranked number #8 in the US Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums (Billboard) chart and number #13 in the US Billboard 200 chart.

His 13th mixtape, 2 Heartless, releases on February 14, 2018. It ranks number #16 on the US Billboard 200 chart.

Moneybagg Yo has also worked as a featured artist in several songs which include the likes of All of a Sudden by Lil Baby, Tomorrow by Stunna 4 Vegas, Unjudge Me by Calboy, Don’t Even Trip by Tee Grizzley, Hocus Pocus by DJ Kay Slay and Blueface, and Set by Dee Mula.
