Who is Chloe Bailey?

Chloe Bailey is an American singer and actress. She is one half of the R&B duo Chloe x Halle with her sister Halle Bailey.

Chloe BaileyAge, Parents, Siblings, Nationality, Ethnicity

She was born on July 1, 1998, in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. Her birth name is Chloe Elizabeth Bailey. She was born to her mother Courtney Bailey and her father Doug Bailey. She has two siblings, a younger sister named Halle Bailey and a younger brother.

Chloe holds American citizenship and her ethnicity is African-American.

Chloe Bailey: Professional Life, Career

She started her career young at the age of 12. With sister Halle Bailey, she started uploading pop song covers on YouTube under the name Chloe x Halle with her sister, Halle Bailey.

Chloe Bailey’s father left his job in order to become their full-time manager and his daughters then began to perform all over Atlanta.

In September 2013, the sisters released their first EP titled Uncovered under the name ‘Chloe x Halle’. Then, the sisters released their second EP ‘Sugar Symphony’ on  April 29, 2016. In addition, they released a mixtape ‘The Two of Us’, released in March 2017.

The sisters made their breakthrough from their  Beyoncé’s Lemonade visual album as Chloe x Halle was featured in it. In addition, they released their single Warrior which was featured in their debut album ‘The Kids Are Alright, on  March 23, 2018.

Similarly, Chloe Bailey is active in acting career and appeared in ‘The Fighting Temptations’ (2003), ‘Last Holiday’ (2006), ‘Meet the Browns’ (2008), ‘Gospel Hill’ (2009), ‘Let It Shine’ (2012), ‘Joyful Noise’ (2012), Grown-ish (2018-present), and many more.

Awards, Nominations

She has been nominated for NAACP Image Awards (2017), BET Awards (2018), MTV Video Music Awards (2018), MTV Europe Music Awards (2018), Soul Train Music Awards (2018), Grammy Awards (2019).

In February 2019 the sisters graced the cover of Essence magazine.
