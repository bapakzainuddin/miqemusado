Lionel Messi presentation date set with Inter Miami

Lionel Messi’s presentation date has been set as he will be unveiled as a new player of Inter Miami next week.

Messi will be presented as a new player of Inter Miami on Sunday, July 16 at 8:00 pm Eastern at the DRV PNK stadium. Gates for the media will open at 5:00 pm Eastern.
