How Did Marion McCollum Die? President and CEO of Macks Prairie Wings Dies At 81

The passing of Marion McCollum, the esteemed President and CEO of Mack’s Prairie Wings, has left a void in the outdoor sporting community. Known for his passion for hunting, conservation, and entrepreneurship, McCollum’s contributions to the industry were immense. As news of his death on Thursday, June 22nd, 2023 at the age of 81 spread, many were left wondering about the circumstances surrounding his passing. Although Marion is now dead, his legacy will live on through the countless memories and experiences shared by those who were touched by his wisdom and generosity.

Keep reading the article to know more about Marion McCollum, including who he was, how he died, what was the cause of his death, tributes for him, and much more!

Who Was Marion McCollum?

Marion McCollum‘s life was linked with the great outdoors. Born in Stuttgart, Arkansas, in 1942, he grew up in a region known for its rich hunting heritage. From a young age, McCollum developed a deep appreciation for the sport and the natural beauty that surrounded him. He embraced the lifestyle of a hunter and conservationist, fostering a profound understanding of the delicate balance between man and nature.

The Founding of Mack’s Prairie Wings (MPW)

In 1944, Marion McCollum’s parents, founded Mack’s Sport Shop, a small store specializing in hunting equipment and supplies. In 1960, Marion took over the family business and transformed it into Mack’s Prairie Wings, a renowned outdoor sporting goods retailer. Under his leadership, the company flourished, becoming a premier destination for hunters and outdoor enthusiasts across the United States.

McCollum’s steady dedication to his customers and his love for the sport earned him a reputation as a pioneering figure in the hunting industry. His commitment to customer satisfaction and extensive knowledge of hunting gear propelled Mack’s Prairie Wings to new heights. Beyond the commercial success, McCollum was deeply involved in wildlife conservation efforts, supporting organizations such as Ducks Unlimited and the National Wild Turkey Federation.

How Did Marion McCollum Die? Cause of Death Explained

Marion McCollum, a well-liked President and CEO of Mack’s Prairie Wings sadly and unexpectedly passed away on Thursday, 22nd, 2023, at the age of 81.

While the exact cause of his death has not been officially disclosed by his family or the company, it is believed that he succumbed to natural causes.

Given his age, it is not uncommon for individuals to experience age-related health issues. However, it is important to respect the family’s privacy during this difficult time and refrain from speculating on the details surrounding his passing.

The news of Marion McCollum’s demise was first reported by Christy West Johnson, and she also wrote a touchy tribute for him, saying

“Marion McCollum will be sorely missed in my heart after working with him for so many years and having known him for so long! My lovely, sweet friends, my heart aches for you. Jill Flaxman McCollum JoAnn McCollum and Ginger McCollum Holladay. My sincere prayers go out to Mack’s Prairie Wings, who is a legend, as well as to the ladies for courage and the protection of God.”

Tributes for Marion McCollum

In the wake of McCollum’s passing, countless individuals from the hunting and outdoor communities expressed their condolences and shared stories of the impact he had on their lives.

Mack’s Prairie Wings posted on Facebook, saying “We represent the unexpected and heartbreaking news of the demise of our great Marion McCollum, who devoted his life to passing on the traditions of the outdoor sportsman, with heavy hearts. Although Mack’s Prairie Wings might never be the same, we can guarantee you that his legacy will live on as long as we continue to serve customers in the manner that Marion taught us to.”

In a post, Honey Brake mentioned, “Yesterday, Marion McCollum (Mack’s Prairie Wings), a legendary figure in the outdoor business, passed away. Over the years, Marion made a significant impact on numerous young people’s lives and numerous environmental initiatives. His influence on our sector will be missed.”

Original Nut Grub said in a post, “The passing of Marion McCollum has deeply affected everyone at Nut Grub. For those of you who are unaware, Marion was the inspiration behind Mack’s Prairie Wings and one of the best, if not THE BEST, representatives for duck hunting.”

The hunting and outdoor community mourns the loss of Marion McCollum, a true icon in the industry. His dedication to hunting, conservation, and entrepreneurship made a lasting impact on countless individuals. While the specifics of his passing remain undisclosed, it is clear that McCollum’s legacy will endure through the memories and achievements he left behind.
