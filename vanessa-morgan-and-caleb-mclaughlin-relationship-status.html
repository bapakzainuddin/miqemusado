Vanessa Morgan and Michael Kopech Welcomed a Son, River in Early 2021

Vanessa Morgan Mziray also credited as Vanessa Morgan is a multi-talented and famous Canadian actress. She came to stardom for her recurring role Beatrix Castro in the MTV television teen drama, Finding Carter from 2014 to 2015.

 She now portrays Toni Topaz in the drama, Riverdale on The CW. Apart from her career, she is a happily married woman. So today let's talk about her marital life in the given section.

The Outdoor Wedding Ceremony

The 29 years old Vanessa Morgan has tied the knot with her four years younger boyfriend, Michael Kopech, 24. He is an American baseball pitcher who plays for Chicago White Sox under MLB.

They walked down the aisle on January 4, 2020, Saturday at the Walton Historic House in Florida. The intimate and hush-hush ceremony was held in the presence of just 40 guests including the couple's family and closest friends. Also, her fellow co-star Drew Ray Tanner, Skeet Ulrich, and Madelaine Petsch attended their party.

Vanessa looked stunning in an Eisen-Stein Bridal long-sleeved, form-fitting gown with an open back from Israel. For the reception, she changed into another Grace Loves Lace gown. Of course, their conjugal was organized by a wedding planner, Tami Varma.

Morgan and Kopech first became engaged on July 3, 2019, after one year of love romance. The MLB pitcher proposed to her on the trip to Havasu Falls, Arizona and it was a fairy-tale dream, the actress says.

Husband, Michael Filed For Divorce From Vanessa 

Yes, the newlywed's couple Morgan and Kopech were going to end their married life. After six months of marriage, the Chicago White Sox pitcher filed a divorce petition on June 19, 2020, saying court records, The Chicago Tribune stated.

The papers filed in Morris County in his home town Northern Texas but the separation date was not listed, as per sources. The divorce news comes just after days Morgan announced her pregnancy through her official Instagram. 

However, neither he nor she confirmed their divorce. Happily, the couple is now rumoured to be back together and are seen spending time together after welcoming their son in 2021. 

Welcomed a Baby Boy in Early 2021

On July 25, 2020, Riverdale actress announced her pregnancy via Instagram post." She even revealed her baby boy due saying: "Exciting news... I am overjoyed to be welcoming my baby boy into the world this January".

The couple welcomed their son, River Kopech on January 29, 2021. She shared the first snap of her little one baby hand on her IG on 5th February saying the most challenging, beautiful experience of her life. 

She added: "Michael & she Welcomed their beautiful baby boy River into the world".
