Who is JJ Walker?

JJ Walker is a comedian and actor from the United States.

Walker was nominated for a Golden Globe Award in 1975 and 1976 for his role as James Evans Jr. (“J.J.”), the older son of Florida and James Evans Sr., on the CBS television series Good Times, which aired from 1974 to 1979.
