Discovering the Locations of Sages Wills in Tears of the Kingdom

Understanding the Significance of Sage’s Will

Delving into “The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom,” Sage’s Will holds a crucial role in shaping the game’s narrative. This vital concept embodies the profound wisdom and counsel bestowed by the esteemed sages of Hyrule, ancient entities endowed with extraordinary power and knowledge. Sage’s Will serves as a repository of invaluable insights, equipping players with new capabilities to face the encroaching darkness threatening Hyrule’s very foundation. Embarking on the quest to acquire Sage’s Will leads players into a realm of age-old wisdom, unveiling essential keys to restoring harmony and safeguarding the kingdom’s delicate equilibrium.

Locating All Sage’s Wills in Tears of the Kingdom

In “The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom,” uncovering all the Sage’s Wills demands an adventurous spirit, adept problem-solving skills, and keen attention to detail. These sacred fragments of wisdom are scattered across the vast and varied terrains of Hyrule, enticing players on an enthralling scavenger hunt.

To unearth these elusive pieces, players must journey through enchanting forests, treacherous mountains, ancient ruins, and other captivating landscapes comprising the rich tapestry of Hyrule. Engaging with non-player characters, decoding enigmatic clues, and undertaking specific quests are just a few ways players can acquire the coveted Sage’s Wills.

Each revelation brings players closer to unraveling the kingdom’s mysteries and advancing in their noble mission to restore balance and illuminate the land. Prepare for an immersive and thrilling adventure as you embark on the epic quest to discover all the Sage’s Wills and unlock the ancient wisdom they behold.

Discovering the Locations of All Sage’s Wills in Tears of the Kingdom

“The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom” showcases a myriad of locations where players can unearth the Sage’s Wills. Each location presents unique challenges and rewards, enhancing the depth and excitement of the gameplay experience. Some noteworthy locations where players can find Sage’s Wills include the mystical Lost Woods, the ancient Temple of Time, the majestic Death Mountain, the serene Lake Hylia, and the enigmatic Gerudo Desert.

By meticulously exploring these areas and diligently seeking out clues, players can amass all of the Sage’s Wills and harness their power to restore harmony to the kingdom. Below are the locations of all the Sage’s Wills in “The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom”:

  • South Hyrule Sky Archipelago: On a small island with two ponds, utilize Ultrahand to lift a door in the water and attach it to a log, granting access to a chest containing the Sage’s Will.
  • South Eldin Sky Archipelago: Journey to the tiny island in the southwest and utilize ascent or burn vines to access a chamber housing the Sage’s Will.
  • Eldin Sky Archipelago: Look north from the highest point in the South Eldin Sky Archipelago to spot the Eldin Sky Archipelago at a lower altitude. Utilize a Wing Zonai device to reach it.
  • Wellspring Island: Defeat the Flux Construct II miniboss during the main quest in Zora’s Domain to discover the chest containing the Sage’s Will.
  • Sokkala Sky Archipelago: Access the bottom section of a giant floating sphere using Ultrahand to manipulate the sphere. Glide down into the entrance and open the chest to obtain the Sage’s Will.
  • South Hebra Sky Archipelago: On the northern sky island, locate a treasure chest in the center of the ruins containing the Sage’s Will. Launch from Rospro Pass Skyview Tower and glide east to reach this island.
  • Thunderhead Isles: Disperse the thunderstorm in Faron Sky and reach the Thunderhead Isles. Plunder a chest in the center of a pool, then glide to a floating sky island to uncover another chest with the Sage’s Will.
  • Dragonhead Island: Discover a chest on the northwestern tip of Dragonhead Island to retrieve the Sage’s Will. Activate the Joku-u Shrine to fast-travel to this location.
  • North Necluda Sky Archipelago: Launch from Sahasra Slope Skyview Tower and glide northeast to the North Necluda Sky Archipelago. Land on a luminous stone-covered sky island and open the chest for the Sage’s Will.
  • Lanayru Sky Archipelago: Visit the Flux Construct II site and defeat the miniboss or detach the chest using Ultrahand. Launch from Upland Zorana Skyview Tower and glide south to reach this area.
  • Lanayru Great Spring Sky: Launch from Upland Zorana Skyview Tower and glide into the orb-shaped sky island below. Deactivate the spinning mechanism and drop into the lower level to find the Sage’s Will.
  • West Hebra Sky Archipelago: Overcome the Scourge of the Hebra Sky (King Gleeok) on the sky island. Construct an aircraft using Zonai devices to access this island.
  • North Tabantha Sky Archipelago: Head to Strock Lake in Tabantha Frontier, construct an aircraft using Zonai devices, and glide into the waterfall. Ascend the waterfall with the Zora Armor to reach the sky archipelago. Open the chest on the western sky island for the Sage’s Will.
  • Starview Island: Launch from Gerudo Highlands Skyview Tower, land on North Gerudo Sky Archipelago, and glide northeast to Starview Island. Utilize spotlights and Zonai Mirrors to unlock the gate and access the chest containing the Sage’s Will.
  • West Gerudo Sky: Defeat King Gleeok on the southwestern island in Gerudo Sky to unlock a chest with the Sage’s Will. Launch from Gerudo Highlands Skyview Tower and glide southeast to reach this island.
