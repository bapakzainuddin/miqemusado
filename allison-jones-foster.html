Relationship History of Erin's Father, David Foster

David Foster has been married five times to date. Before his marriage, he had a daughter, Allison Jones Foster. He was first married to B.J. Cook. This first marriage ended soon after the wedding. The couple was blessed with a daughter, Amy Skylark, in 1973, who is a well-known Songwriter and an author. After this, He married his second wife, Rebecca Dyer, on October 27, 1982. 

They were in a relationship for some time before exchanging their wedding vows. Like that of the first marriage, this also ended in divorce in 1986. They had three children. Sara, Erin, and Jordan. Then in 1991, He was in love with an actress, Linda Thompson, and exchanged their wedding vows. 

This marriage lasted for fifteen years, and they separated in 2005 with mutual consent. After being single for some time, He married his fourth wife, Yolanda Hadid, a Dutch Model. Like that of his previous marriage, this ended in 2015 after being in a relationship for almost a decade.

Then in June 2018, The Composer got engaged to Katherine McPhee. Then on June 28, 2019, they walked down the aisle at the Armenian Church of St Yeghiche, South Kensington, in London. David is living a happy life, along with his wife.
