Karima Adebibe Net Worth


Karima Adebibe has accumulated a net worth of $2 million. She is known for her work as a Moroccan and English actress as well as a fashion model. Adebibe was born on February 14, 1985, in Bethnal Green, United Kingdom, and stands at a height of 5 ft 8 in (1.74 m). She has been involved in various movies and TV series such as “Fearless,” “Vikings,” “Sherlock Holmes: A Game of Shadows,” and “AVP: Alien vs. Predator.”

In February 2006, Adebibe was announced as the new Lara Croft model, making her the 7th model to take on the role. Although born in London, she is of Moroccan and Irish descent through her parents.

Adebibe’s impressive career has also included appearances in documentaries like “10 Years of Tomb Raider: A GameTap Retrospective” and TV series like “HypaSpace.” Her talent and contributions in the entertainment industry have established her as a notable figure in the world of acting and modeling.
