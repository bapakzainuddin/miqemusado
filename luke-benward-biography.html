Who is Luke Benward?

Luke Benward is an American actor and singer. People mostly know him for his role as Billy Forrester in ‘How to Eat Fried Worms’ and as Charlie Tuttle in ‘Minutemen.’

Additionally, he has also appeared in several other movies and television series including ‘Life of the Party’, ‘Still the King’, ‘Field of Lost Shoes, and ‘Good Luck Charlie’ among others.

Luke Benward: Age, Parents, Siblings, Family

Benward was born in Franklin, Tennessee on May 12, 1995. He was born to parents Kenda and Aaron Benward. His mother is a part-time actress and his father is in the country duo Blue County.

Additionally, he has two younger sisters, Gracie and Ella. Luke is of American nationality. Furthermore, he belongs to a mixed ethnic background of English, German, and Scots-Irish/Northern Irish. Luke’s grandfather Jeoffrey Benward, is a Contemporary Christian music singer.

Luke Benward: Awards, Nominations

Luke Benward won the Young Artist Award in 2007 for ‘How to Eat Fried Worms’ in the category of Best Young Ensemble in a Feature Film.

Additionally, he also received the nomination for the same award in 2009 for ‘Minutemen’ in the category of Best Performance in a TV Movie, Miniseries, or Special – Leading Young Actor.
