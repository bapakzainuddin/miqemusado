Get to know Malu Trevejo: age, biography, height, net worth, boyfriend, and latest updates



Get the latest updates on Malu Trevejo’s age, biography, height, net worth, boyfriend, and much more!

Malu Trevejo, also known as Mara Luisa Trevejo, is a talented Cuban-American singer-songwriter.

Malu Trevejo: Age, Biography, and Profile Summary

Malu Trevejo, born on 15th October 2002, is currently 20 years old. She hails from a wealthy Catholic Christian family in Havana, Cuba. With Cuban-American nationality and a strong belief in the Christian faith, her ethnicity is Cuban, and her zodiac sign is Libra.

She completed her early education at a local high school in Spain and is yet to pursue higher education. From a young age, she showed a keen interest in dancing and extracurricular activities, overshadowing her academic pursuits.

Malu Trevejo: Family, Boyfriend, and Relationships

Her father, Mr. Trevejo, is a businessman, while her mother, Mrs. Trevejo, is a homemaker. She has one sister, and currently, she is not in a relationship, having previously dated thatboiipapii, Swae Lee, Central Cee, Jaden Delarosa, and NBA Youngboy.

Malu Trevejo’s Education

Malu completed her elementary education in Madrid, Spain, and pursued her secondary education at a prestigious public high school in Florida.

Malu Trevejo’s Career

Upon moving to the United States, Malu focused more on her Instagram and TikTok presence, sharing singing and dancing videos. Her popularity soared as she gained millions of followers and was recognized as a social media influencer. this led to record deals with Universal Music Latin and In-Tu Linea when she was just fourteen years old.

In September 2017, she released her debut single, Luna Llena, which was a massive hit and garnered over 113 million views on YouTube. Her debut EP, Una Vez Más, was released two years later, further solidifying her position in the music industry.

She continued to bless her fans with captivating singles, such as Hasta Luego, Nadie Como Yo, and Swipe Dat in 2018, followed by Como T Me Quieres, Down For Your Love, and Think About in 2019. In 2020, she switched management teams and relocated to Los Angeles.

Malu Trevejo’s Net Worth

Malu has become one of the world’s most successful young Latino artists, accumulating significant wealth from her music career, brand endorsements, and influencing opportunities. Her net worth is estimated to be between $500,000 and $750,000.

She gained fame through her engaging content on Musical.ly and has garnered a massive following on Instagram, boasting more than 10 million followers.
